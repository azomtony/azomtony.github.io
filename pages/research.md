<section class="page-hero">
  <p class="eyebrow">Research direction</p>
  <h1 class="page-title">Molecular insight for complex interfaces</h1>
  <p class="page-summary">
    My research uses molecular simulation, machine-learning force fields, and enhanced sampling
    to understand structure, dynamics, and transport at solid-liquid interfaces and in confined channels.
  </p>
</section>

<section class="section-block expertise-band">
  <div class="expertise-grid">
    <div>
      <h3>Solid-liquid interfaces</h3>
      <p>Graphene oxide, graphene, water, methanol, and membrane interfaces where local chemistry controls structure and reactivity.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h3>ML force fields</h3>
      <p>Deep-learning potentials trained from quantum data to bridge electronic accuracy and molecular-dynamics timescales.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <h3>Nanofluidic transport</h3>
      <p>Water, ion, and proton transport through confined pores, including functionalized CNT porins and responsive gates.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
